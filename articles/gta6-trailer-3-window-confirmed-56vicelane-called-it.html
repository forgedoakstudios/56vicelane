What Zelnick Actually Said

Following the Take-Two earnings call on May 21, CEO Strauss Zelnick sat down with Bloomberg for a follow-up interview. When asked directly about the GTA 6 marketing timeline he was precise about what "summertime" means in this context.

"So the next few weeks I don't think it'll be summertime yet, but when it's summertime, Rockstar expects to start marketing GTA 6."

When pressed further on what he meant by summertime, he clarified — end of June or early July. Summer in the United States officially begins June 21. Zelnick ruled out everything before that date. The marketing campaign, Trailer 3, and pre-orders all fire together at the same moment — and that moment is not before June 21.

What This Rules Out

No Trailer 3 at Summer Game Fest on June 6. No surprise May drop. No early June reveal. Every leaker who predicted a May or early June trailer was wrong. Zelnick put a hard floor on the timeline himself — June 21 at the absolute earliest, with late June to early July being the confirmed window.

Why The World Cup Matters

The FIFA World Cup runs through July 19, 2026, with the final on July 19. Take-Two and Rockstar have no incentive to compete with the most-watched sporting event on the planet for media attention. Launching a global marketing campaign for the biggest entertainment release in history while 5 billion people are watching football makes no strategic sense.

This creates a natural pressure window — launch the marketing campaign before the World Cup consumes all available attention, or wait until after July 19. Given Zelnick's "end of June or early July" language, the most logical window is late June, before the tournament reaches its knockout stages and dominates global media.

June 25 to July 8 sits in exactly that sweet spot — summer has officially started, the World Cup group stages are wrapping up, and Rockstar can own the news cycle before the tournament's final weeks take over.

Pre-Orders And Pricing — Same Window

Zelnick confirmed on the earnings call that pre-orders will go live as soon as marketing starts. He also confirmed that pricing will be announced alongside the marketing launch — not before. Every question about how much GTA 6 costs gets answered the same day Trailer 3 drops.
