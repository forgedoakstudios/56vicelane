GTA6 Release Date Locked: November 19, 2026 Is Official

After two delays and years of anticipation, Rockstar Games officially confirmed Grand Theft Auto VI launches November 19, 2026 on PS5 and Xbox Series X/S. This is not a leak. Not a rumor. Straight from Rockstar's official Newswire.

By 56ViceLane Staff  ·  April 28, 2026

The Official Confirmation

Rockstar Games published the November 19, 2026 release date on their official Newswire at rockstargames.com — the same platform where every previous delay and announcement has been made. The announcement came with a statement apologizing for the additional wait and confirming the game needed more time to meet the standards fans expect.

"We are sorry for adding additional time to what we realize has been a long wait, but these extra months will allow us to finish the game with the level of polish you have come to expect."

— Rockstar Games, Official Newswire

Parent company Take-Two Interactive corroborated the date through their investor communications. When two sources at that level agree, the date is locked. CEO Strauss Zelnick has since doubled down that November 19 is not moving again.

The Delay History

Dec 2023
GTA6 officially announced with Trailer 1 and a target window of 2025.
Early 2025
First delay announced — game pushed from Fall 2025 to Spring 2026 with a May 26, 2026 date set.
Nov 2025
Second delay announced — minutes before Take-Two's earnings call, the game was pushed from May 26, 2026 to November 19, 2026.
May 2026
November 19 holds — CEO Zelnick confirms the date is locked. Pre-orders expected imminently.

What Launches November 19

GTA6 launches on PS5 and Xbox Series X/S only. There is no PC version announced. Based on Rockstar's history — GTA5 came to PC about 19 months after console — a PC release in 2027 or 2028 is plausible but unconfirmed. PS4 and Xbox One are not in the picture.

Both digital and physical editions are confirmed for launch day. Pricing has not been officially announced — the expected range based on CEO comments is $70–$80 for the standard edition.

Confirmed Facts — Release Date
  • November 19, 2026 — official launch date from Rockstar Newswire
  • PS5 and Xbox Series X/S — only confirmed platforms at launch
  • Digital and physical editions both available day one
  • No PC release date — historically arrives 18+ months after console launch
  • No PS4 or Xbox One — current-gen only
  • GTA Online — not confirmed at launch, expected post-release

Why The Date Won't Move Again

Multiple signals point to November 19 being final. Take-Two's earnings call on May 21 puts the CEO in front of investors who have been told this date twice. A third delay would be catastrophic for the stock and credibility. Major publishers have already maneuvered their release calendars around Rockstar's November window — treating it as an immovable obstacle. The infrastructure for pre-orders is being built.
