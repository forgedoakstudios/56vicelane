The Biggest Trailer Launches In History — Where GTA6 Actually Ranks

📅 July 22, 2026 ✍️ 56ViceLane News Desk ⏱️ 5 min read 🔄 Updated as new records fall
📘 Facebook 𝕏 Twitter 🔴 Reddit 🔗 Copy Link

Trailer view counts have turned into their own sport, and the leaderboard just moved again. Avengers: Doomsday dropped its first trailer on July 20 and racked up 503 million views in 24 hours — Disney's biggest trailer debut ever, ahead of GTA6's own record-setting reveal, and behind only one thing on the planet. We're keeping a running tally of where every major reveal actually lands, updated every time a new one drops.

Two things jump out. First, three of the four biggest trailer debuts in history all happened within the same rough stretch of 2026 — this genuinely is an unprecedented moment for trailer-as-event culture, not just a GTA6 story. Second, and more importantly for anyone who follows this site: GTA6 remains the single biggest video game reveal of all time, and it's still outperforming or matching movie trailers with vastly larger built-in franchise audiences behind them. A game beating superhero-movie-sequel numbers is not normal, and it hasn't happened before.

🎬 Why Doomsday Specifically Popped

The trailer dropped July 20 — deliberately timed several days ahead of Marvel's Comic-Con presentation, giving it a full news cycle to build momentum before the studio's biggest stage moment of the year. That timing move is exactly the kind of marketing playbook Rockstar itself has used for GTA6's own reveals.

🎮 What This Actually Means For GTA6

Nothing changes about GTA6's own numbers — this is Marvel setting a movie-trailer record, not a game-trailer record, and the two aren't directly comparable in any way that affects GTA6's standing in its own category. What it does confirm is the broader pattern: 2026 is shaping up as the single biggest year for trailer-as-cultural-event moments industry-wide, gaming included. If anything, that raises the stakes for whatever Rockstar drops next between now and November 19 — the bar for "biggest reveal of the year" just got set by two different studios in the same summer.

The bottom line

Spider-Man still holds the all-time record. Doomsday is now clearly #2 and Disney's best ever. GTA6 sits right behind both of them — and ahead of everything else that's ever been released, gaming or otherwise. We'll keep updating this leaderboard every time a new trailer threatens to move it, because at this rate, it won't sit still for long.
