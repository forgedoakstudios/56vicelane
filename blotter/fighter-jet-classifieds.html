Man Arrested For Attempting To Sell A Fighter Jet On A Local Classifieds Site

@LSOddities·Filed under: things that definitely happened

A listing titled "JET (fighter) — runs, some rust, no trades" appeared on a local classifieds site Sunday, priced at two thousand dollars, negotiable, cash only, local pickup only, described in full as: "Cool jet. Was going to fix it up but life got busy. Comes with the little ejector seat thing. Buyer must be able to move it."

The listing included four photos, one of which clearly showed the aircraft still bearing partial military markings, taken, investigators believe, on a private airstrip the seller does not own and has no documented connection to.

Officers arrived to find the man mid-negotiation with an interested buyer who had, remarkably, already brought a flatbed truck. When asked where the jet came from, the seller reportedly said, "That's kind of a long story, and also I'd rather not," which detectives noted was "not the reassurance we were hoping for."

The jet has been returned to its rightful, extremely relieved owner, who was previously unaware it had left the premises. The would-be buyer has asked whether anything else is available. He has been advised no.

The seller faces multiple charges. The listing, mercifully, has been removed. Several screenshots have not.

— Filed by an officer who screenshotted the listing before it got taken down
