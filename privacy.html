Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Who We Are

56ViceLane is an independent fan site operated by Forged Oak Studios. We are not affiliated with Rockstar Games or Take-Two Interactive. Contact: forgedoakstudios@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you sign up for The Last Drive event, purchase a license plate, or contact us. This includes:

  • Gamertag (PSN ID, Xbox Gamertag, or Steam name)
  • Email address
  • Gaming platform and region
  • Purchase information (processed by PayPal — we do not store card details)
  • Optional profile information (about text, profile picture URL)

We also collect information automatically through analytics, including pages visited, time on site, device type, and approximate location (country/region level). We do not collect precise GPS location.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display your gamertag on the Wall of Honor (if you opt in)
  • Process your license plate purchase and apply it to your profile
  • Send event updates related to The Last Drive (if you opt in)
  • Respond to contact form submissions
  • Improve site performance and content through analytics
  • Display relevant advertising through Google AdSense

4. Google AdSense

56ViceLane uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information on how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use 56ViceLane. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Amazon Associates

56ViceLane participates in the Amazon Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. When you click an Amazon link on this site and make a qualifying purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Amazon uses cookies to track referrals. See Amazon's Privacy Notice for details.

7. Airtable

We use Airtable as our backend database to store Last Drive signups, purchase records, and contact form submissions. Data stored in Airtable is subject to Airtable's Privacy Policy. We do not share your data with third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the site.

8. PayPal

License plate and merchandise purchases are processed by PayPal. We do not store your payment card information. All payment data is handled by PayPal and subject to PayPal's Privacy Statement.

9. Cookies

56ViceLane uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics uses cookies to track site usage
  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads
  • Functionality: We use localStorage to remember debug mode preferences
  • Affiliate tracking: Amazon Associates uses cookies to track referral purchases

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

10. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services. Last Drive signup data will be retained through November 19, 2026 and may be archived afterward as a permanent record of the event. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing forgedoakstudios@gmail.com.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at forgedoakstudios@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

12. Children's Privacy

56ViceLane is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

13. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of 56ViceLane after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact

For privacy questions or requests: forgedoakstudios@gmail.com
