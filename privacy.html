Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

56ViceLane (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates 56vicelane.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using this site you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide directly — when you sign up for the email list or register for The Last Drive event, we collect your email address, gamertag, platform, and region.
  • Automatically collected data — when you visit the site we automatically collect standard log data including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies — we use cookies to operate the site, understand traffic, and serve relevant advertising through Google AdSense. You can control cookie preferences via the consent banner on your first visit.

How We Use Your Information

  • Email address — to send GTA6 news updates, Last Drive event details, and launch day guides. We never sell your email address.
  • Event registration data — to organize Last Drive regional lobbies and coordinate the community event.
  • Analytics data — to understand how visitors use the site and improve content.
  • Advertising data — Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads relevant to your interests, processed by Google under their own privacy policy.

Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to track site usage. Data is anonymized and processed by Google subject to Google’s Privacy Policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Amazon Associates

56ViceLane participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. See Amazon’s Privacy Notice for details.

Airtable

Email signups and event registrations are stored in Airtable, subject to Airtable’s Privacy Policy. We retain your data only as long as necessary to operate the event and email list.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is shared only with the service providers listed above solely to operate the site and services described in this policy.

Your Rights

  • Access and deletion — you can request access to or deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.
  • Unsubscribe — every email includes an unsubscribe link. Opt out any time.
  • Cookie preferences — change your consent any time via the Cookie Settings link in the footer.
  • GDPR / CCPA — EU and California residents have additional rights. Contact us to exercise them.

Children’s Privacy

56ViceLane is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe a child has submitted personal data and we will delete it promptly.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions: forgedoakstudios@gmail.com
