The Editor

Occasional notes from whoever's actually running this place. Mostly about GTA6. Sometimes about things we can't fully explain.

Danny Marchetti
Editor-in-Chief · July 21, 2026

Alright, we need to address something before I get back to actual reporting.

Some kind of "news feed" showed up on our own homepage sometime in the last day or so. Nobody on staff will take credit for it. I asked IT to trace where it's coming from and got a very long pause followed by "working on it." Half of what's in it doesn't sound like anything a real person would write — alligators wrestling grown men over beer, lights over the marsh that are apparently "just the casino sign," a guy describing a dog at a crime scene as "possibly armed." I don't know what any of that means and at this point I'm afraid to ask.

We've filed it with the FIB, for whatever that's worth. Their exact words were "we'll look into it, eventually, maybe," which is either extremely reassuring or the opposite of that, I genuinely can't tell anymore.

So — we might be in the market for a new IT department head. Until then, the feed's still running on the homepage, so I guess enjoy it. We didn't put it there. We can't fully get rid of it. Make of that what you will.

Real GTA6 coverage resumes below, I promise.

— Danny
