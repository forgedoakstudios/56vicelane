How The Last Drive Works
⭐ For Drive Leads
Sign up and claim your slot — platform, region, time, and staging location
Your gamertag goes on the Wall of Honor — share it so riders can find and add you
Before November 18: add all riders from your drive to your friends list in-game
On the night: stage at your location, enable Passive Mode, invite your crew to a session
Rules: No killing · No chaos · No grieving · Just the road
🚗 For Participants
Browse available drives, pick one that matches your platform, region, and time
Join the drive — your name goes on the wall under your Drive Lead
Add your Drive Lead to your in-game friends list RIGHT NOW — don't wait until November 18
On the night: your Drive Lead will send you a session invite at your agreed time
Enable Passive Mode before you join — keep the drive clean
🔑 The Golden Rule
Add every person in your drive to your friends list before November 18. Session invites only work if you're friends in-game. Don't wait until the night — do it now.
