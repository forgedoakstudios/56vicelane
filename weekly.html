Editor-in-Chief · The Recap

Trevor
Staff Voice, Unofficially · The Rest Of It

THIRTEEN YEARS. Danny says thirteen years like it's a fun fact. Buddy, I've done deep stretches shorter than that and come out the other side a changed— anyway. Doesn't matter. Point is the waiting is the hard part and everybody feels it.

You want the real news from this week? The Crime Blotter on the homepage is more accurate than anything the FIB will confirm on the record, and I'd know, I— — was that a boat? Somebody's moving a boat down the interstate again. Focus. Okay. Focus.

Where was I. Right. Everybody's freaking out about a release date like it's the only thing that's ever mattered, and honestly? Fair. But while you're waiting, go grab a nameplate, sign up for the Last Drive, yell at me in my inbox. Do something with your hands. Trust me, sitting still for a decade waiting for one specific day to arrive does things to a man. Hypothetically. I read that somewhere.

Danny's gonna edit this down and pretend he didn't. He won't. He's tired. Look at the little picture of him, he's already more tired than last week. That's the countdown doing that. Nov 19 can't come fast enough for either of us and only one of us will admit it.

— T
